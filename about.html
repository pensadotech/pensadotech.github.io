About me

My name is Armando A. Pensado and I have been a software engineer for many years now. Long time ago, I fell in love with the human intellect capable to find new and better ways to do things and gain understating of the universe around us. This love leaded me to be involved in technologies in early stages of my life.

I have always been fascinated with science and technology, looking to learn new concepts that can be used in real-world applications. One of my favorite authors that shaped my youth was Carl Sagan. The book that impacted me the most is “Dragons of Eden”. On those days, it was a book full of revolutionary ideas and fresh look into what it means to be human. I read most of his books and watched the show “Cosmos”.

As a software engineer, one thing I can share is that this career keeps you in alert all the time, solving problems is a main role. After all, IT professionals are engineers, and solving problems is part of the job description. The IT profession is an ever-changing world, and that is an interesting concept for people passionate about it. It is never boring.

Interest and Activities

As a person, you may think is weird, but my interest has been around learning something new every year, about techniques, technologies, and scientific ideas. I like to observe how other people come with new ways to solving things or scientist discovering a new concept. I like to read books, watch scientific documentaries in PBS, or find articles in the internet with interesting ideas. Have you read before the book “We have no idea” by Jorge Cham and Daniel Whiteson? Well, that is a book I could not put down.

Other forces that shaped my life were playing sports, outdoor activities, and traveling with the family. I have numerous adventures in different terrains, and I treasure each one of them. These activities show me a world of wonders and at the same time make me humble and appreciate our wonderful planet. I really try to do my small part to save the planet and I hope you do too.
