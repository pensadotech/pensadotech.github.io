Welcome!

Welcome to my GitHub pages! In here you will find applications that I like to share with the world, part of my personal projects or coding exercises. My hope is to share examples to help other developers learn or compare notes. I never consider myself an expert, as software technologies is an ever-changing world. Nevertheless, by sharing the way I came to a solution, can spark new ideas for the next generations.

Games, a fun way to learn

As a software developer, I have been exposed to all kind of projects with different levels of challenges. However, i found through people that have shared knowledge with me, that making games is a great way to learn and practice programming.

Within my pages you will find some of the games I created for the web, and these are responsive in design and can be played in computers and portable devices like tables and phones.

In my portfolio page, I offer the link to the page to play the game or the location for the sources inside the GitHub community. My hope is that you find them at least educational.

Projects

In the GitHub community, Opensource code is a strong philosophy about collaboration and learning by "doing things". This is connected with the “Makers” movement in many parts of the world. Chris Anderson, founder of 3D Robotics, expressed in his book, “Makers” is the new industrial revolution: “Computing and Communications are also ‘force multipliers’, doing for services what automation did for manufacturers” (@2012, ‘Makers’ the new industrial revolution).

Chris Anderson is founder of 3D Robotics, a company better known for their fantastic Drones, that worked under the Opensource principle all the time. He considered this a must, and a fundamental force to keep us improving every time.

The projects ideas shared within these GitHub repositories are inspired by the philosophy of Opensource and Makers.
